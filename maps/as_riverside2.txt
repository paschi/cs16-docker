England, May 15th
An important European business man
is the target of a Terrorist Group.
CTs have been called to escort
him to a near town from where he
will get out of England.

Counter-Terrorists: 
Escort the VIP to one of two safety 
zones where the jeeps are waiting. Team 
members must protect the VIP by all means.

Terrorists:
Kill the VIP!
